\section*{Ex 1.6}
\begin{mdframed}
	let $\Sigma = \varnothing$.
	\[
		|2^{\Sigma^*}| \;-\; ?
	\]
\end{mdframed}

$\Sigma^* = {\varepsilon} \cup \bigcup_{i=1}^\infty \varnothing = {\varepsilon}$.
Let $L_1 \subseteq \Sigma^* = {\varepsilon}$.
$L_1 = \varnothing$ or $L_1 = {\varepsilon}$.

So, $\displaystyle |2^{\Sigma^*}| = 2$
